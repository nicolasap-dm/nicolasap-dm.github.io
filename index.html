I'm Nicola De Mitri *

I live in Cambridge, UK, and I'm a scientific software developer at Enthought.

I work with Python on consulting projects involving data management and visualisation.

This website is a work in progress!

research

Until recently, I have carried out research in computational chemistry at Scuola Normale Superiore (Pisa, Italy) and at the University of Cambridge, working on the development of classical and coarse-grained models for the simulation of organic molecules, based and parametrised on DFT calculations.
